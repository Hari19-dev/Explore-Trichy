EXPLORE TRICHY

ROCKFORT

Rockfort is a historic fortress and temple complex situated atop a massive rock outcrop, offering stunning views of the city and housing ancient temples and structures. This place is the heart of Trichy. Popularly known as the Rockfort, it is the main symbol for Trichy.

Visit

KALLANAI

Kallanai, also known as Grand Anicut, is an ancient dam located in Trichy. Constructed by the Chola king Karikalan around the 2nd century AD, it is one of the oldest water-diversion structures still in use.

Visit

SRIRANGAM

Srirangam, located in Trichy, is a renowned temple town in India, famous for its spectacular Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple, one of the largest functioning Hindu temples in the world.

Visit

AGAYA GANGAI

"Agaya Gangai" is a captivating waterfall known for its beauty and serenity, located in border of Trichy. Its cascading waters and lush surroundings create a mesmerizing natural spectacle, drawing visitors from far and wide to witness its splendor.

Visit

MUKKOMBU DAM

The mukkombu dam, also known as the Upper Anaicut, is a notable water structure located in Trichy, Tamil Nadu, India. This dam is renowned for its scenic waterfalls that captivate visitors with their beauty and serenity.

Visit

PULIYANCHOLAI

Puliyancholai is a breathtaking waterfall located in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Nestled amidst the lush greenery of the Kolli Hills, the waterfall cascades down the rocky terrain, offering a mesmerizing sight and a serene atmosphere for visitors to enjoy and unwind.

Visit

PACHAIMALAI HILLS

The Pachaimalai Hills are adorned with numerous picturesque waterfalls that cascade down its lush green slopes, creating a mesmerizing sight for visitors. These waterfalls offer a refreshing and tranquil escape amidst the natural beauty of the region.

Visit

BUTTERFLY PARK

The "Butterfly Park" features enchanting a big butterfly that add to the park's picturesque beauty. The cascading water flows gracefully, creating a serene and soothing ambiance, making it a perfect spot for visitors to immerse themselves in nature's tranquility.Perfect spot to visit with friends, family and couples.

Visit

AASAI ULAGAM THEME PARK

"Aasai Ulagam Theme Park" is still not opened and construction process are about to complete. The park's lush green surroundings and the refreshing sound of water falling from various heights offer a serene and exhilarating ambiance.
